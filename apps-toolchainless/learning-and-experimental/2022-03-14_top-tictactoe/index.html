This web app follows Project: Tic Tac Toe from The Odin Project, including the AI extension criteria.

This app wasn't designed with mobile in mind.

Four different AI players are implemented:

  • Hard: Plays perfectly by taking advantage of openings for guaranteed wins, or forcing a draw if there isn't one. Implemented using the Minimax algorithm.
    • This AI may sometimes choose not to take a win on the next turn, but (I believe) it will only do so if it can guarantee a win in subsequent turns anyway.
  • Medium: During each turn, there's a 60% chance of playing like Hard mode, and a 40% chance of playing like Easy mode.
  • Easy: Picks any square at random. All empty squares have equal probabilities of being chosen.
  • Loser: Tries to lose. Similar to Hard mode, it will take advantage of openings for guaranteed losses, or forcing a draw if there isn't one. Also implemented using the Minimax algorithm.
